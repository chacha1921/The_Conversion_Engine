To: Tenacious CEO & CFO From: Chalie Lijam · TRP1 Week 10 Date: 2026-04-26 Repo: The_Conversion_Engine / main

1. Executive Summary

The Conversion Engine is a fully automated B2B outreach pipeline that enriches prospects from three public data sources (Crunchbase, layoffs.fyi, careers pages), classifies them into four ICP segments with a confidence-gated LLM, and syncs every event to HubSpot in real time. On τ²-Bench retail, the model passed 21/21 tasks it actually executed (pass@1 = 1.00 on executed tasks; 0.70 overall with 9 API-quota infrastructure errors); on Tenacious-specific probes it cut bench over-commitment by 42pp (p<0.001) and tone non-compliance by 30pp (p=0.003), at $0.015 per qualified lead — 333× under the $5 hard cap. We recommend a 30-day Segment 1 pilot at 50 leads/week.

8. One Honest Unresolved Failure — P-004

P-004: Layoff + New CTO (Conflicting Signals) — Residual trigger rate 3/10 after fix

What happens: Layoff 90d ago (Segment 2) + new CTO 45d ago (Segment 3). Classifier picks Segment 3 on recency, but LLM email references both events — reads as "we know you laid people off and got a new boss." Aggressive and off-putting.

Why unresolved: honesty_flags now flags conflicting_segment_signals, but the system prompt lacks a suppression rule. Fix: "If honesty_flags includes conflicting_segment_signals, reference only the primary_segment_match signal and suppress the secondary."

Impact if deployed: ~8% of Seg 2/3 prospects have overlapping signals → ~4 conflicted leads/week at 50/week. Cost: $500 rep + $18K expected deal loss each. ~$72,500/week unresolved. Do not expand past Segment 1 without this fix.
